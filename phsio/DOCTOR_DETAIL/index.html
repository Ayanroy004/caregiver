About

Orthopedic physiotherapy focuses on the diagnosis, management, and rehabilitation of conditions related to the musculoskeletal system, which includes bones, joints, muscles, ligaments, and tendons. It is commonly used for patients recovering from injuries such as fractures, sprains, and strains, or following surgeries like joint replacements and ligament reconstructions. The aim is to restore mobility, strength, and function to the affected area.
type of physiotherapy is also beneficial for individuals dealing with chronic conditions like arthritis or postural problems. Through a combination of exercises, manual therapy, and sometimes modalities like heat or ultrasound therapy, orthopedic physiotherapists work to improve movement, reduce pain, and prevent further injury. They also provide patient education on posture, movement mechanics, and injury prevention techniques, ensuring long-term recovery and maintenance of optimal physical health.
